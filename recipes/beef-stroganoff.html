Beef Stroganoff

Description

A delicious(?) recipe of beef stroganoff found in the credits of the game HROT.

What You Need

  • 500g Sirloin Steak
  • 200g Button Mushrooms
  • 2dl Sour Cream
  • 1dl Red Wine
  • 1 Can Tomato Paste
  • 1 Onion
  • Pickled Cucumbers
  • Butter
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the meat into strips.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Lightly fry the onion in butter.
  4. Add the meat strips and fry them on all sides.
  5. When the meat is fried, add sliced mushrooms, tomato paste and fry them for a moment.
  6. Pour in sour cream and red wine.
  7. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Serve with rice, garnish with a scoop of sour cream and cube-shaped slices of cucumber.
